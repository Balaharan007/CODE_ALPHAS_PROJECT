About SoftSkills Analyzer

SoftSkills Analyzer is an advanced tool that helps you improve your communication skills by analyzing your facial expressions, voice tone, and speech patterns.

The system uses machine learning models to evaluate your emotional expression, speech sentiment, voice pitch, and intensity to provide a comprehensive soft skills assessment.

Based on the analysis, the tool offers personalized suggestions to help you enhance your communication effectiveness in professional and personal settings.
